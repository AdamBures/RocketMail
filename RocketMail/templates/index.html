Who we are?

RocketMail is a technology company that is revolutionizing the email industry with its Web3 email service. Our goal is to create a decentralized email service that provides users with greater control and ownership over their emails, while also leveraging the security and transparency of blockchain technology. We believe that email is a critical communication tool that should be secure, private, and accessible to everyone. With RocketMail, we are making this vision a reality by offering a simple, user-friendly email service that is powered by the latest Web3 technologies. Join us on our mission to revolutionize the email industry and take control of your email experience.
